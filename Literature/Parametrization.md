# Parametrization

## Definition

**Definition**. A _parametrization_ of a line $c:[0,1]\to\mathbb{R}^n$ passing through a point $p=(p^1,\ldots,p^n)$ with direction $v=[v^1,\ldots,v^n]$  is given, for every $t\in[0,1]$, by $c(t)=(p^1+tv^1,\ldots,p^n+tv^n)$.
