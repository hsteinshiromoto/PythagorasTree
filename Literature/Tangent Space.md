# Tangent Space

## Definition

**Definition**. Let $p$ be a point of $\mathbb{R}^n$ , the _tangent space_ at $p$, denoted as $T_p(\mathbb{R})$ is the set of vectors emanating at $p$. The elemetns of $T_p(\mathbb{R})$ are said to be _tangent vectors_.
