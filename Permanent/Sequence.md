# Sequence

## Definition

**Definition**. Let $X$ be a set, a sequence in $X$ is a map from the set $\mathbb{N}$ of positive integers to $X$. A sequence is denoted as $\{x_i\}_{i\in\mathbb{N}}$ or $x_1,x_2,x_3,\ldots$
