# Uniqueness of the Limit

## Proposition

**Proposition**. In a [[Hausdorff Topological Space|Hausdorff space]] $X$, if a [[sequence]] $\{x_i\}_{i\in\mathbb{N}}$ converges to $p$ and to $q$, then $p = q$.
