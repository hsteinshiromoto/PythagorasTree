# Open Ball

## Definition

**Definition**. Let $(X, d)$ be a [[metric space]]. For any $p\in X$ and $r>0$, the set $$B_{<r}(p)=\{x\in X|m(x,p)<r\}\;$$ is said to be an _open ball_ with center $p$ and radius $r$.
